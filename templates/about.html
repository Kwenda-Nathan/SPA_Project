About Us

EncryptionSim is dedicated to providing a hands-on learning experience for cryptography enthusiasts. Our platform helps users explore and understand encryption techniques such as the Caesar cipher and XOR encryption.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify cryptographic concepts, making them accessible to students, developers, and security researchers through interactive simulations.

Our Vission

We aim to be a leading educational platform for encryption techniques, helping individuals build a solid foundation in cybersecurity.

Our Achievements

  • Developed an interactive encryption simulation tool.
  • Made it possible for students grasp cryptography fundamentals.
